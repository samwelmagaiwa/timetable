4. Business Rules (CRITICAL)
4.1 Weekend Rules (Saturday & Sunday)
Exactly:
1 Morning staff
1 Evening staff
Night:
1 or 2 staff (configurable, default = 2)
4.2 Night Shift Rules
If a staff works Night:
Next day:
Cannot work Morning
Can only work Evening (or Off)
If a staff works 2 consecutive Nights:
Must get:
2 consecutive OFF days
Cannot be scheduled during rest
4.3 Rest Enforcement
Staff in rest period:
Cannot be assigned any shift
4.4 Shift Constraints
One staff → one shift per day
No overlapping shifts
4.5 Coverage Constraints

Each day must have:

Required shifts filled (no gaps)
5. Fairness Constraints
Each staff must have:
Minimum 8 night shifts
Maximum 10 night shifts
Workload must be balanced across staff
6. Scheduling Algorithm (MANDATORY IMPLEMENTATION)

Implement a constraint-driven scheduling engine with the following structure:

STEP 1: Initialization
Create empty schedule matrix:
schedule[staff][day]
Track:
night_count per staff
rest_until per staff
STEP 2: Assign Night Shifts FIRST (Highest Priority)

For each day:

Determine number of night staff:
Weekend → 1–2
Weekday → 1 (configurable)
Filter candidates:
Exclude:
Staff in rest
Staff exceeding night limits
Staff with 2 consecutive nights
Rank candidates:
Lowest night_count
Longest since last night shift
Assign night shift(s)
Update:
night_count
last_night_day
STEP 3: Apply Night Constraints

For each night assignment:

Next day:
Disallow Morning
If 2 consecutive nights:
Day+2 and Day+3 → OFF
Update rest_until
STEP 4: Assign Weekend Shifts (M + E)

For each weekend day:

Assign:
1 Morning
1 Evening

Constraints:

Exclude:
Previous night staff
Staff in rest
Already assigned staff

Selection:

Choose least workload staff
STEP 5: Assign Weekday Shifts

For weekdays:

Assign Morning first, then Evening

Constraints:

No Morning after Night
Respect rest rules

Selection:

Least total workload
STEP 6: Gap Filling
Fill any unassigned shifts
Relax only soft constraints
Never violate hard constraints
STEP 7: Optimization Phase

Run iterative improvement:

Swap shifts between staff
Evaluate using scoring function:

Score =

Night imbalance penalty
Workload imbalance penalty
Rule violation penalty (very high)

Keep only improvements
